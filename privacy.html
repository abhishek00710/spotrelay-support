SpotRelay Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 23, 2026

SpotRelay is designed to help drivers share parking handoffs and remember parked car locations. This policy explains what information the app may use to provide those features.

Information We Use

SpotRelay may use location, motion activity, vehicle connection signals such as Bluetooth or CarPlay, and parking handoff activity to detect parking moments, show nearby parking spots, and help complete handoffs.

Location Data

Location data is used to show nearby parking handoffs, save parked car locations, provide directions back to your car, and complete claimed handoffs when a user arrives near a shared spot.

Sharing

When you share or auto-share a parking spot, the app may publish approximate parking spot information so nearby users can claim it. SpotRelay does not sell your personal information.

Notifications

SpotRelay may send notifications related to parking reminders, spot sharing, claimed spots, and handoff activity.

Data Retention

Recent parking history and active handoff information may be stored to support app features. You may remove saved parking information from within the app where available.
